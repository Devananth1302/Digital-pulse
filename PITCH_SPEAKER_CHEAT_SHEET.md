## 🎯 COMMON STUMBLES (Avoid These!)

❌ **Don't:**
- Rush through numbers (pause 1.5 seconds after each key stat)
- Say "um" or "uh" (silence is better)
- Over-explain technical details (keep Slide 3 brisk)
- Repeat the same phrase twice (every sentence should advance)

✅ **Do:**
- Emphasize compound words: "**Real-time** insights", "**Hierarchical** clustering"
- Smile at Slide 1 opening and Slide 5 closing (bookends)
- Vary your pace (slow on problems, medium on solutions, fast on numbers)
- Pause between slides (1 second = lets transitions happen)
